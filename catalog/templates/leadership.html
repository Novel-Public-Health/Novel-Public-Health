Founder

Novel was founded on the campus of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill by a Master of Public Health candidate at the renowned Gillings School of Global Public Health. Since its inception, Novel has been owned and operated by public health professionals. At Novel our staff is united behind our vision to be the leading platform for public health films across the globe. Special thanks goes to all the alumni, faculty, and staff at UNC who have contributed valuable consultation towards the creation of Novel. The UNC innovation and entrepreneurship community has been a truly inviting family for embracing Novel with open arms. Thank you to all who contributed. Novel will forever be grateful for your efforts.

Team

Currently, Novel’s team consists of an extraordinarily talented group of interns from UNC’s Department of Computer Science. Novel plans to utilize the UNC Chapel Hill community as a resource for future internship and fellowship opportunities for students. As Novel expands, there is a growing need for various positions. Position openings will be posted on our site soon. Interested in Joining Us? Fill out the form on the contact us page for more inquiries.
