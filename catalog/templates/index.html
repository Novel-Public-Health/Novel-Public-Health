What is Novel?

Novel is an innovative and multidimensional public health media company that explores public health through film. It functions as a video streaming, digital media, and online networking company dedicated to expanding the public health film industry. Here at Novel, we focus on highlighting urgent public health concerns in the local, national, and global community. To our knowledge, we are the first of its kind

Why was this established?

Novel was created in response to the novel coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic. The pandemic has highlighted now more than ever, the importance of public health and stability of public health systems. Modern public health strategies are geared towards cross sector collaboration and interdisciplinary approaches. With this same intent, Novel is focused on the intersection of media, film, and public health. As a result, Novel is dedicated to increasing health literacy, public health awareness, and advocacy for complex public health issues

What is our intent?

Novel features inventive, groundbreaking, and engaging motion pictures to the public. We are invested in films that stretch the imagination, understanding, and vision of public health. All films hosted on Novel are authentic pieces capable of catching the attention of audiences across the globe

What is the objective?

Our Mission is to promote public health research, innovation, and entrepreneurship through film

"Recent, Remarkable, Revolutionary" -- this is Novel

What do we envision?

We envision Novel becoming the leading platform for public health films in the world

Our Work: What are we doing?

Novel promotes public health to cultivate a health conscious, competent, and safe population. Our platform deepens the perception of public health, thereby emphasizing the need for urgent action to prevent looming crises and heal legacies of injustice. Furthermore, Novel illuminates solutions-- some may be fanstical scientific developments, some may require bureaucratic action, and others may demand organizing to implement swift policy changes-- but all are united in their bold innovation and optimism about the future. We believe that highlighting these audacious ideas and the valiant leaders behind them will drive the field of public health, and the health of our global community forward.

Our Goals: What do we hope to accomplish?

Novel does more than highlight the gutsy innovators driving public health forward, it fosters horizontal connection among them, and induces conversation about impending health crises. As we expand, we envision a whole new genre of emerging film. As innovators, we are motivated to curate realistic films with a Hollywood experience. We see a future of public health that is ever changing and adaptive. In this future, we are the founders of tomorrow.
